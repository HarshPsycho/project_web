Code Career Camp is the world’s largest and fastest-growing kids coding franchise, with hundreds of locations in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. Our goal is to create the problem solvers of tomorrow. At our centers, kids learn to code while building their own video games in a fun, safe, and inspiring environment. Kids gain confidence, logic, resourcefulness, and problem solving skills that will serve them for the rest of their lives.
